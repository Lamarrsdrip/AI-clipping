FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Do I need editing experience to use ClipForge?

No. You paste a YouTube URL or upload a file. ClipForge handles everything — finding the best moments, framing, captions, and formatting for each platform automatically.

What types of videos work best?

Podcasts, interviews, tutorials, YouTube vlogs, long-form videos, and live streams all work great. ClipForge finds the moments that stop scrollers regardless of content type. Faceless channels can use the Faceless Studio to create content from scratch.

How does the face tracking work?

Our Reframe Engine v6 uses MediaPipe AI to detect every face in every frame. It predicts speaker movement with 0.4-second velocity lead, applies Gaussian smoothing for cinematic motion, and supports 7 framing modes — from close-up to group shot.

Can I use ClipForge for my faceless YouTube channel?

Yes — and it's one of ClipForge's strongest features. The Faceless Studio lets you type any topic and get a complete script, scene directions, B-roll keywords, voiceover cues, SEO title, video description, and hashtags — for 22 different content niches.

What AI providers power ClipForge?

ClipForge is AI-provider agnostic. It supports xAI Grok, OpenAI GPT-4o-mini, Groq Llama, and any OpenAI-compatible API. For video/image generation it uses Kling, Higgsfield, FLUX, Seedance, and Wav2Lip. For voice, ElevenLabs TTS is integrated.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. The Free plan gives you 15 credits every month with no credit card required. That's enough to create your first clips and see what ClipForge can do. Upgrade only when you're ready.
